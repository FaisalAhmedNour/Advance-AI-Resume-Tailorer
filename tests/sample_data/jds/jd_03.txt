Staff Frontend Engineer — Consumer Product
Location: Austin, TX (Hybrid)
Type: Full-time

About the Role:
We build productivity tools used by millions of consumers. Our frontend team creates fast, accessible, and delightful user experiences. We're looking for a Staff Frontend Engineer to set the technical direction for our web platform.

Responsibilities:

- Partner with product and design teams to ship high-quality features from Figma to production.

Required Skills:
- 6+ years of frontend engineering experience.
- Expert in React, TypeScript, and modern CSS (Tailwind CSS or CSS-in-JS).
- Deep understanding of browser rendering, performance profiling, and Core Web Vitals.
- Experience building and maintaining design systems or component libraries.
- Hands-on with testing frameworks (Jest, Cypress, Playwright).

Preferred Skills:
- Familiarity with Next.js for SSR/SSG.
- Experience with Storybook for component documentation.
- Knowledge of animation libraries (Framer Motion, GSAP).

Soft Skills:
- Strong leadership and mentoring ability.
- Excellent cross-functional collaboration.
- Clear technical communication (written RFCs, ADRs).
